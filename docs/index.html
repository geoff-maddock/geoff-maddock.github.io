Summary

Geoffrey hails from Pittsburgh, PA. He earned a degree in Computer Science from the University of Pittsburgh. His professional career has been entirely under the roof at Expedient, a mid-sided datacenter hosting and managed services company. After getting his feet wet in Technical Support and Network Operations, he became a full time Programmer, focusing on improving the company infrastructure and building robust web applications for all aspects of the operation. His work has always been ad-hoc coding and finding the best tool for the job, but his specialty has become designing and building custom, effective applications in the Symfony framework with PHP, Javascript and MySQL and Oracle backends. In parallel, for many years he ran an online record distro, which paired with regular event promotion and both live and dj-based club nights. He enjoys sci-fi, art, music, gaming and the outdoors.
